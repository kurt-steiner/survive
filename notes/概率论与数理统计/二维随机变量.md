# 二维随机变量及其分布

## 二维离散型随机变量及其概率分布

|$X \setminus Y$ | $y_1$| $y_2$| $y_3$| $y_4$| 
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| $x_1$| $p_{11}$| $p_{12}$|$p_{13}$ | $p_{14}$|
| $x_2$| $p_{21}$| $p_{22}$|$p_{23}$ | $p_{24}$|

随便写一个表出来，根据书上的定义，对 $(X = x_i, Y = y_j)$  
我们有 **联合分布**
$$
P(X = x_i, Y = y_j)
$$

对于 $X=\text{input(x)}$ 的**边缘分布**，有  
$$
\begin{aligned}
&&P(X = \text{input}(x))& \\
&&= P(X = \text{input}(x), Y = \text{all}(y))& \\
&&= P(X = \text{input}(x), Y = y_1) + P(X = \text{input}(x), Y = y_2)&\\
&&+ P(X = \text{input}(x), Y = y_3) + P(X = \text{input}(x), Y = y_4)& \\
&&=\text{sum}(P(X = \text{input}(x), Y = \text{all}(x)))&
\end{aligned}
$$

同样的，对于 $Y=\text{input(Y)}$ 的**边缘分布**
$$
P(Y = \text{input}(y))\\
 = \text{sum}(P(X = \text{all}(x), Y = \text{input}(y)))
$$

对于 $X = x_1 | Y = y_1$ 时的**条件分布**，有
$$
\begin{aligned}
&P(X = x_1 | Y = y_1)&\\
& = \frac{P(X = x_1, Y = y_1)}{P(X = x_1, Y = y_1) + P(X = x_2, Y = y_2)}&\\
\end{aligned}
$$

我们可以推导出，对于 **条件分布** $P(X = \text{input}(x) | Y = \text{input}(y))$ 有
$$
\begin{aligned}
&&P(X = \text{input}(x) | Y = \text{input}(y))&\\
&& = \frac{P(X = \text{input}(x) | Y = \text{input}(y))}{\text{sum}(P(X = \text{all}(x), Y = \text{input}(y)))}&
\end{aligned}
$$

## 二维连续型随机变量及其概率分布

类似的，我们随便弄个表格
|$X \setminus Y$ | $y_1$| $y_2$| $y_3$| $y_4$| $\dots$
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| $x_1$| $f(x_1, y_1)$| $f(x_1, y_2)$|$f(x_1, y_3)$ | $f(x_1, y_4)$| $\dots$|
| $x_2$| $f(x_2, y_1)$| $f(x_2, y_2)$|$f(x_2, y_3)$ | $f(x_2, y_4)$| $\dots$|
| $\dots$|$\dots$|$\dots$|$\dots$|$\dots$|$\dots$|

对于 $X = \text{input}(x)$ 的**边缘概率密度**，有
$$
\begin{aligned}
&f(X = \text{input}(x))& \\
& = \text{sum}(f(X = \text{input(x)}, Y = \text{all(y)} ))&\\
&= \int_{-\infin}^{+\infin} f(\text{input}(x), y) dy&\\
&=f_X(X)&
\end{aligned}
$$

同样的，对于 $Y = \text{input}(y)$ 的**边缘概率密度**，有
$$
\begin{aligned}
&f(Y = \text{input}(y))&\\
& = \text{sum}(f(X = \text{all(x)}, Y = \text{input}(y) ))&\\
&= \int_{-\infin}^{+\infin} f(x, \text{input}(y)) dx&\\
&=f_Y(Y)&
\end{aligned}
$$

对于 $X = x_1, Y = y_1$ 时的 **条件概率密度**，有
$$
\begin{aligned}
&f(X = x_1 | Y = y_1)&\\
& = \frac{f(x_1, y_1)}{f(Y = y_1)}& \\
& = \frac{f(x_1, y_1)}{f_Y(y_1)}&
\end{aligned}
$$
